NPO Start Dual Subtitles

Translation is activated automatically when you open a video. Use the button in the video player controls to toggle it on or off. Subtitles are translated using Google Translate.

Click a Dutch subtitle word to pause the video and show its translation.

Makes subtitle words selectable/hoverable so external tools (like Reverso) can translate words directly from the video page.
